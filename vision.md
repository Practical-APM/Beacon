# Beacon

## Vision Document v1.0

### Working Tagline

Datadog for Customer Implementations

### One-Line Vision

Help B2B companies predict, explain, and prevent implementation delays before revenue is impacted.

---

# Problem Statement

Modern B2B companies invest heavily in acquiring customers but still struggle to successfully onboard and implement them.

The implementation process typically spans multiple teams:

* Sales
* Customer Success
* Solutions Engineering
* Product
* Engineering
* Security
* Procurement
* Customer Stakeholders

Information is fragmented across:

* CRM
* Project Management Tools
* Slack
* Email
* Support Systems
* Documentation Platforms

As implementations become more complex, leaders lose visibility into:

* Which projects are at risk
* Why delays occur
* Which dependencies are blocking progress
* What revenue is impacted
* What actions should be taken

Organizations spend millions on dashboards, project management software, and reporting systems, yet they still rely on manual status meetings to understand implementation health.

The result:

* Delayed go-lives
* Slower revenue realization
* Customer frustration
* Increased churn risk
* Operational inefficiencies

---

# Our Core Belief

Businesses do not need another project management tool.

Businesses need an intelligence layer that continuously monitors implementation health and proactively identifies risks before they become problems.

Project Management answers:

"What happened?"

Beacon answers:

"What is about to happen?"
