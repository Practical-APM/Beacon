# Executive Summary

Beacon is an Operational Intelligence platform focused on helping B2B companies identify implementation risks before revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency are impacted.

The platform integrates with existing business systems, continuously analyzes implementation activity, and proactively surfaces:

* Implementation risks
* Root causes
* Revenue impact
* Recommended actions

The initial market focus is enterprise SaaS, fintech, HRTech, ERP, and integration-heavy companies.

---

# Business Problem

## Current State

Organizations invest significant resources in acquiring customers but lack visibility into implementation execution.

Customer implementations are managed across:

* CRM systems
* Project management tools
* Communication platforms
* Ticketing systems
* Internal documentation

As implementations scale:

* Delays become common
* Visibility decreases
* Executive reporting becomes reactive
* Revenue realization slows

Most organizations discover issues after deadlines have already slipped.

---

## Consequences

### Financial

* Delayed ARR recognition
* Delayed expansion revenue
* Reduced implementation capacity

---

### Customer

* Increased frustration
* Reduced trust
* Lower adoption
* Higher churn probability

---

### Operational

* More status meetings
* More escalations
* Increased management overhead

# Opportunity

Organizations spend heavily on:

* CRM
* Project Management

Yet there is no dedicated intelligence layer focused on implementation risk prediction.

The opportunity is to become:
