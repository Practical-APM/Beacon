# Positioning Statement

For B2B SaaS, FinTech, and implementation-heavy companies struggling with delayed customer onboarding and implementation projects, Beacon continuously identifies implementation risks, predicts delivery delays, and explains root causes before revenue is impacted.

Unlike project management tools that track work, Beacon tells leadership what is likely to go wrong and what actions should be taken.
